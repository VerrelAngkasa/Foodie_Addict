Most Popular Drinks

by Foodie Addict, September 2024

#2 Jamu

A traditional herbal drink made from natural
ingredients like turmeric, ginger, tamarind,
lemongrass, and other herbs. Known for its
health benefits, particularly for boosting immunity,
improving digestion, and reducing inflammation.

Price Range: IDR 3 - 10K

#1 Es Cendol

A refreshing iced dessert drink made with
green rice flour jelly, coconut milk, and
palm sugar syrup. It has a sweet
and slightly savory flavor with
a hint of pandan aroma.

Price Range: IDR 10 - 20K

#3 Kopi Luwak

Known as one of the most famous coffees
in the world, this coffee is made from beans
that have been eaten and excreted by civet cats,
giving it a unique flavor. It is known for its
smooth, rich, and less bitter taste.

Price Range: IDR 10 - 25K
